🍌 The Totally True Origin Story of Trusty Bananas Inc.*

Long ago, in a small village known only as Peeltonia, a boy named Chadwick B. Banana had a dream:

“What if... bananas were more than just a fruit? What if they were... a lifestyle?” At the tender age of 12, Chadwick slipped on a rogue banana peel during gym class. While unconscious, he had a vision of a banana-shaped spaceship piloted by a wise monkey in a lab coat. The monkey whispered: “Trust... the banana.” When Chadwick awoke (to mild applause and a detention slip), he dedicated his life to banana innovation. He dropped out of school, moved into a treehouse, and began the Great Peel Experiments—attempting to turn bananas into everything from socks to Wi-Fi routers. Most of the experiments failed. Except one. A prototype called B.A.N.A.N.A. (Brilliant And Nutritionally Awesome New-age Agriculture) It was just a banana... but it came with a tiny hat. People loved it. Thus, in 1999, Trusty Bananas Inc. was born—with a mission to deliver the world's most reliable, fashion-forward bananas.

🍌 Notable Company Milestones

  • 2003 – First banana delivered via drone. It exploded on impact, but it was awesome.
  • 2011 – Briefly pivoted to cryptocurrency: Bananacoin. It was literally just regular coins inside bananas. Didn't go well.
  • 2017 – Launched the “Peel with Pride” campaign. Sales soared. So did monkey attacks.
  • 2022 – Acquired by a consortium of vegan billionaires and parrots.
  • 2025 – Unveiled “BananaGPT,” an AI that only responds with banana facts and compliments.

Today, Trusty Bananas Inc. is a global potassium empire with 14 employees, 46 billion bananas in circulation, and one pending lawsuit with the country of Ecuador.

Their motto?

"In Bananas We Trusty."
